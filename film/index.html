History of the First Motion Picture

The history of motion pictures dates back to the late 19th century, with the earliest experiments in moving images taking place in the 1870s and 1880s. While there is some debate among film historians about which specific film should be considered the first motion picture, there are several early films that played an important role in the development of cinema.
